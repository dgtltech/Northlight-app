## What's new in 1.5

This release adds SVG support.

### Added

- **SVG support (`.svg`, `.svgz`).** Simple SVGs render as true vectors via CoreSVG — sharp at any zoom level, with transparency shown over the background. SVGs that use features beyond that (embedded or external images, filters, stylesheets, scripts) automatically render through WebKit with browser-grade fidelity and re-sharpen shortly after you zoom. SVG works everywhere other formats do: folder navigation, drag & drop, the status bar, Settings → Defaults, and the "Fix Quarantine in Folder…" button.
